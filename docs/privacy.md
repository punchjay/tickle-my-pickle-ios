# Privacy Policy — Tickle My Pickle

**Effective date:** July 9, 2026

Tickle My Pickle ("the app"), published by Denova LLC, is an iOS app for
finding pickleball courts near you. It is built to collect as little data as
possible: no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking.

## What the app collects and why

**Your location (optional).** If you tap **"Near me"**, the app asks iOS for
permission to use your location while you're using the app. Your coordinates
are used once per search to find courts nearby. Location access is never
required — you can always type a city, ZIP, or neighborhood instead — and the
app never accesses your location in the background.

**Your search text.** Whatever you type into the search box (a city, ZIP, or
neighborhood) is used to look up that place.

## Where that data goes

To perform a search, the app sends your search text or coordinates to
**Google's Geocoding and Places APIs**, which return matching locations and
nearby pickleball courts. Google processes those requests under the
[Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). The app sends
nothing else — no name, email, device identifiers, or advertising IDs.

## What stays on your device

Saved (favorited) courts are stored only on your device. They are never
uploaded anywhere, and deleting the app deletes them.

## What we don't do

- No accounts or sign-in — we can't identify who you are
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs
- No selling or sharing of personal data
- No background location access

## Children

The app is rated 4+ and collects no personal information from anyone,
including children.
